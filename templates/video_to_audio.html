How to Use the Video Converter

Step 1: Upload Your Video

Select the video file you wish to convert by clicking the "Choose file" button above. Supported file formats include MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, and FLV.

Step 2: Choose a Format

After selecting your video, choose the audio format you want to convert it to (MP3, WAV, ACC, FLAC).

Step 3: Convert and Download

Once you've selected the format, click "Convert to audio". Your video will be processed, converted to audio and then will automatically download to your device.
